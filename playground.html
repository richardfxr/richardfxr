Kyle loves pink and blue. This should come as no surprise to his viewers. But, pink and blue is a rather challenging color scheme to work with. After multiple iterations, we ended up with an unusual yet pleasing combination that is different from other YouTube channels.

➀ Ⓛ

The final logo is a stylized CPU heatsink. A total of 6 logomarks were created. Most of the logomarks are inspired by computer hardware such as PSU cables or PCIe slots. But, my favorite has to be the one based on Kyle’s head. There were also 7 logotypes, some of which were styled to match the logos.
