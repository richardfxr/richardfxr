Orbis Rounded is a rounded geometric typeface. Born out of my frustration with the lack of perfectly circular geometric fonts, Orbis Rounded goes to great length to keep its characters circular.

Ⓐ

Keeping everything perfectly circular limits Orbis Rounded’s character set. This is why the typeface only features lower-case letters. Additional glyphs and characters are still in the works.

Orbis Rounded is meant to be used as a heading typeface. Its heavy weight and rounded nature excel on large billboards or posters.

Ⓐ
Ⓑ

Other weights are still in the works. I’m hoping to turn Orbis Rounded into a full-fledged typeface one day.
